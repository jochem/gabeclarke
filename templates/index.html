Gabe Clarke — Dutch-American tenor

Gabe

Tenor

Clarke

Praised for a "beautiful and lyrical sound" and a "mature interpretation and fine control" (Place de l'Opéra), tenor Gabe Clarke is increasingly recognized for his technical command and stylistic elegance, particularly within the bel canto repertoire.

Critics have noted his "vocal brilliance" and agility in the works of Rossini and Bellini, marking him as a rising artist of significant promise. His ability to marry nuanced phrasing with the technical demands of the genre has made him a sought-after interpreter of this tradition. The 2025–2026 season saw a significant professional milestone with his debut at the prestigious Wexford Festival Opera, where he performed the leading tenor role of Il cavalier Belfiore in Rossini's Il viaggio a Reims under the direction of Rosetta Cucchi.
