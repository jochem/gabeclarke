Ofelia is a multidisciplinary Designer, currently working at Fouroom Inc. and specializing in brand identities, editorial design and web.

She creates design for brands of all shapes and sizes through Art Direction, Branding, and Illustration. She works independently for a variety of clients in Tech, Fashion, Beauty, and Culture.
