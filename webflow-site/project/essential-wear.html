JOhan Strauss

DIe Fledermaus

Dutch National Opera Academy
A collaboration with the Residentie Orkest
An operetta in three acts by Johann Strauß
Libretto Karl Haffner, Richard Genée

"Apart from the excellent ladies, ... there’s Gabe Clarke as Alfred à la Damiano David from the Eurovision Song Contest band Måneskin. They all have a hearty go at operetta and come out, at the end of three turbulent acts, triumphantly."

The Dutch National Opera Academy's recent production of Die Fledermaus was a resounding success, offering a fresh and exciting take on this beloved operetta. Performed at the iconic Compagnietheater in Amsterdam, the production captivated audiences with its unique blend of energy, talent, and atmospheric setting.

‍

‍

The talented cast of young professionals delivered powerhouse performances that showcased their exceptional skills and enthusiasm. Each singer brought their own unique interpretation to their roles, creating a dynamic and engaging ensemble. The Residentie Orkest provided a dynamic and supportive accompaniment, bringing Strauss's score to life with their vibrant playing.
